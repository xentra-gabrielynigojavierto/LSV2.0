# LS-TENANT-004 — Access & Explainability (Cross-Entity Visibility)

## Objective

Deliver a **unified Access & Explainability layer** in the tenant portal that allows tenant admins to:

* understand **who has access to what**
* understand **why access exists**
* trace **access across users, groups, roles, and permissions**
* identify **policy impacts and restrictions**

This feature transforms the system from **manageable** → **fully explainable and auditable**.

At completion, tenant admins must be able to answer:

```text
Who has access?
Why do they have it?
Where does it come from?
What is restricting it?
```

## Architectural Principles

### Single Source of Truth

* MUST reuse LS-COR-AUT APIs:

  * access-debug
  * policy evaluation
  * effective access resolution

### No Derived Authorization Logic in UI

* UI must render backend truth, not compute access

### Explainability First

* every permission must show:

  * source (role/group/direct)
  * inheritance path
  * policy impact

# 5. Policy Impact Visualization (ABAC Integration)

---

## Purpose

Expose ABAC decisions to tenant admins.

---

## UI

Within user access + permission views:

### Show:

* policy evaluated
* allow/deny
* rule results
* deny override indicator

---

## Example

```text
Permission: SYNQ_FUND.application:approve

Policy:
- approval.limit (Deny)
- Rule: amount < 50000 → FAILED

Result: DENIED
```

---

## Data Source

Reuse:

* access-debug
* policy evaluation outputs
