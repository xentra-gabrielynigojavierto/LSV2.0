Feature ID: LS-NOTIF-SMS-016

Title:
Recipient Intelligence, Suppression Intelligence, and Delivery Reputation Management

Objective:
Enhance the SMS platform with recipient-level operational intelligence including delivery reputation scoring, invalid-number detection, retry suppression heuristics, destination risk scoring, carrier failure analytics, and suppression intelligence to reduce wasted spend and improve delivery quality.

Critical Architectural Constraint:
Notification Service remains the source of truth for:
- recipient intelligence
- delivery reputation scoring
- suppression heuristics
- retry suppression decisions
- carrier failure analytics
- destination risk analytics
- recipient intelligence APIs

Control Center must only consume Notification Service APIs and render/administer operational visibility. Do not duplicate scoring, suppression, or intelligence logic in Control Center.

Requirements:
- Implement recipient intelligence inside Notification Service.
- Implement suppression heuristics inside Notification Service.
- Preserve all existing compliance/preference enforcement from LS-NOTIF-SMS-002.
- Preserve all routing/provider orchestration from LS-NOTIF-SMS-014 and LS-NOTIF-SMS-015.
- Do not store raw phone numbers in intelligence persistence.
- Use deterministic hashing/tokenization for recipient identity where persistence is required.
- Preserve all existing outbound send behavior unless suppression heuristics explicitly intervene.
- Suppression behavior must degrade safely when telemetry is insufficient.
- Keep Control Center as read-only operational/admin UX.
- Preserve all LS-NOTIF-SMS-001 through LS-NOTIF-SMS-015 behavior.

Implementation Requirements:
1. Add recipient reputation/intelligence persistence.

STEP 6 — Add Retry Suppression Evaluation
Integrate suppression intelligence into retry/send evaluation.

Suggested interface:
ISmsRetrySuppressionService

Suggested methods:
- EvaluateAsync(request, ct)

Suggested request:
- recipient hash
- tenantId
- provider
- retry count
- failure category
- prior suppression history

Expected outputs:
- allow
- warn
- soft_suppress
- hard_suppress
- review_required

Behavior:
allow:
- existing retry/send behavior unchanged

warn:
- allow retry/send but persist warning decision

soft_suppress:
- suppress retry temporarily but preserve audit/dead-letter handling

hard_suppress:
- block retry/send and persist suppression decision

review_required:
- suppress automated retry and require operational review

Requirements:
- Existing retry/dead-letter architecture remains intact.
- Suppression should intervene before retry execution.
- Suppression must not crash delivery pipeline.
- If telemetry insufficient, default to allow or warn.
- Suppression actions must be auditable.
