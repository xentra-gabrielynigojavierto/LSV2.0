STEP 3 — DEFINE CUSTOMER ACCESS MODEL:

Customer access must allow only:
- List own CustomerVisible tickets
- Get own CustomerVisible ticket
- Add comment to own CustomerVisible ticket

Customer access must NOT allow:
- Queue access
- Assignment
- Status management
- Internal-only tickets

- Cross-tenant tickets
- Admin/agent endpoints
- Product reference management unless explicitly safe and scoped
